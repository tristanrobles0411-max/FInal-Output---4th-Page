MISSION

Our mission is to provide reliable, durable, and affordable bicycles that cater to the needs of everyday riders in the Philippines. We are committed to promoting safe and efficient transportation by offering high-quality bike products and services that support both beginners and experienced cyclists. Through continuous improvement and customer-focused service, we aim to ensure that every rider experiences comfort, safety, and satisfaction in every journey they take with our bicycles.

VISION

Our vision is to become one of the most trusted and recognized local bicycle brands in the Philippines, known for delivering long-lasting quality, honest service, and innovative cycling solutions. We aim to build a strong cycling community that encourages healthy living, environmental awareness, and active lifestyles. By continuously improving our products and services, we strive to be a brand that riders can depend on for both daily transportation and professional cycling needs.

ABOUT US

ARES Bikes is a locally established business based in Imus, Cavite that specializes in providing quality bicycles, cycling parts, and accessories. Our company was built with the goal of making cycling more accessible, practical, and affordable for Filipino riders. We focus on delivering products that combine durability, performance, and value for money. Over the years, we have served a growing number of customers who trust us not only for our products but also for our commitment to customer satisfaction and dependable service.

HISTORY

ARES Bikes was founded in 2014 as a small family-run business located in Anabu, Imus, Cavite. It started with a simple passion for bicycles and a goal to provide affordable and reliable bikes to the local community. From a small shop operation, the business slowly expanded through hard work, consistency, and the trust of its customers. As demand grew, ARES Bikes developed into a more established brand that continues to serve cyclists of different levels. Today, it stands as a growing local business dedicated to supporting the cycling community in Cavite and nearby areas.
